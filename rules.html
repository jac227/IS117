Rules of Bowling

On the surface bowling seems like a simple sport and in a way it is. The main rule most people already know is "do not cross the line". That line is the foul line, and it is placed at the start of the lane. If any part of your body crosses that foul line you will hear a buzzing sound and receive a score of F meaning foul and is counts the same as a 0.
